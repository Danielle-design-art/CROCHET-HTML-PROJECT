CROCHET TODAY Y'ALL!!!

Crocheting can be a fun and rewarding hobby as you create something beautiful and most likely useful as well. With just a little patience and expected errors along the way, you too can enjoy your hand crafted pieces as well as have some things to pass on to your loved ones.

There are such a variety of crochet projects from 1 hour projects for instant gratification to 1 year or more for those willing to wait a bit longer for the end result. Amigurumi or crocheted "stuffies" such as crocheted animals and dolls are sometimes worked in pieces and sewn together with finer yarns creating a more delicate piece. These "stuffies" can also be crafted using thicker and thereby quicker yarn which are equally beautiful,but just not as "dainty". The possibilities are practically endless with what you can crochet, from clothing to blankets as well as home decor.

Classes are available M-F as requested and are flexible to suit your needs. Kits below are for individual projects and contain everything you need to start and complete a project.
~Happy Crocheting:)
